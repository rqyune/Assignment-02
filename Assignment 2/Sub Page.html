Why is
Financial Literacy
so Important?

Using a credit card and building a credit score is essential
to an everyday, independent life and future because it involves
concepts like budgeting, saving, and responsible spending.
But what about the real-world applications?

Real world applications of Financial Literacy

  • 01 Interest

    Credit cards charge interest if you don’t pay the balance due, interest adds up over time turning a small amount into a large debt. Learn how interest works and the importance of paying on time.

  • 02 Debt

    Financial literacy will help you understand that a credit card is borrowed money, not extra income. If you know how to budget and track your spending, you’re less likely rack up more than you can pay back

  • 03 History

    Using a credit card responsibly helps build a strong credit history, it'll allow you to quality for loans, rent housing or for financing large purchase such as a car.

  • 04 Fees

    You want to avoid any extra fees added to your balance, understand the terms of your. credit card to avoid unnecessary costs.
